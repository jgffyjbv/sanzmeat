Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

1. Introduction

Sanz Meat (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information including:

  • Contact information (name, address, phone, email)
  • Order information
  • Payment information
  • Website usage information for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide our products and services and fulfill orders
  • Process payments
  • Respond to inquiries and communicate with you about your orders or account
  • Improve our services and website experience
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements

Text Messaging (TCPA Compliance)

By providing your phone number, you agree to receive SMS communications from Sanz Meat at the phone number provided. These may include Account Payment reminders or program messages. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You can opt out at any time by replying STOP or, for assistance, reply HELP.

We do not send marketing or promotional messages without your express consent.

4. Information Security

We implement appropriate technical, administrative, and physical safeguards to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, use, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our security measures include encrypted data transmission, secure storage systems, and limited access protocols.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number, email address, or personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your information is only used to provide our services to you and to comply with legal or regulatory requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to your personal information
  • Request restrictions on how we use or disclose your information
  • Receive confidential communications
  • Withdraw consent for SMS/email communications at any time
  • File a complaint if you believe your privacy rights have been violated
